Remembering a Tech Innovator

Steve Jobs was an America entrepreneur, businessman, investor, and industrial designer. He saw the pontential of what Pixar could be before the rest of us, and beyond what anyone ever imagined. Steve took a chance on us and believed in our crazy dream of making computer animated films; the one thing he always said was to 'make it great.'

Here's a time line of Steve Job's life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California.
  • 1974 - Jobs took a position as a video game designer with Atari.
  • 1976 - Started Apple Computers with Steve Wozniak.
  • 1985 - Jobs left the company to begin a new hardware and software enterprise called NeXT, Inc.
  • 1986 - Jobs purchased an animation company from George Lucas, which later became Pixar Animation Studios.
  • 2003 - Jobs discovered that he had a neuroendocrine tumor, a rare but operable form of pancreatic cancer.
  • 2004 - Jobs had a surgery to remove the pancreatic tumor.
  • 2006 - The Pixar Animation studios merged with Walt Disney in 2006, making Steve jobs Disney's largest shareholder.
  • 2009 - Jobs health insues returned.
  • 2011 - on October 5, 2011, Apple Inc. announced that its co-founder had passed away.
